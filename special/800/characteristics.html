Minako's characteristics, laid out below and unique to her own personality. Thus, some of these characteristics will apply to Minako only, not to the other senshi. Also note that these descriptions are mostly based on the manga version of Minako.

GIRL
Spunky. Happy. Energetic. Spontaneous. Kind. Friendly. Adorable. Open. Loving. Boy-crazy. Humorous. Sweet. Dreamful. Care-free. Futuristic. Unique. Imaginative. Wishful. Vibrant. Libra.

FIGURE
Idol. Hero. Soldier. Star. Leader. Responsibility. Duty. Sacrifice. Inspirational. Brave. Cunning. Strategic. Devoted. Powerful. Masquerade.

BEAUTY (outer)
Elegance. Exquisite. Magnificance. Lady-like. Feminine. Majestic. Mystical. Angelic. Innocent. Graceful. Pure. Eternal. Princess. Goddess.

BEAUTY (inner)
Childish. Secretive. Hidden. Insecure. Troubled. Truthful. Forgiving.
